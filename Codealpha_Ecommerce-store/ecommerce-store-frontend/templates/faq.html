Trust & Verification Hub

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our 21-point hardware verification, nationwide delivery in Nigeria, secure payment options, and warranty coverage.

Are all your iPhones, MacBooks, and gadgets 100% original and factory sealed?
100% Genuine Guarantee

Yes. Every single device sold on Nexara is 100% original, brand new, and factory unsealed/sealed directly from authorized global distributors. We do not sell refurbished, cloned, or "Lagos-used" devices unless explicitly marked in our certified pre-owned section. Prior to dispatch, our technical team runs a strict 21-point hardware verification check validating IMEI and serial numbers directly against Apple, Samsung, and ASUS official manufacturer databases.

Can I verify the IMEI or Serial Number before making full payment?

Absolutely. If you select Pay on Delivery within Lagos or Abuja, our dispatch courier will allow you to verify the physical security seal, check the device IMEI on official verification portals (`checkcoverage.apple.com` or Samsung warranty verification), and inspect the box before you authorize payment via terminal card transfer or cash.

Do you accept Pay on Delivery (PoD) in Lagos and Abuja?
Verified Payment Gateway

Yes! We offer zero-commitment Pay on Delivery across all metropolitan areas of Lagos and Abuja FCT. For nationwide orders outside these zones (e.g., Port Harcourt, Kano, Enugu, Ibadan), we require either secure online card payment via Paystack/Flutterwave or a direct bank transfer confirmation prior to dispatch through our insured logistics partners.

What payment methods do you accept online and upon delivery?

We accept:

  • Direct Bank Transfer: Instant automated verification into our corporate bank accounts (GTBank, Zenith Bank, Access Bank).
  • Debit & Credit Cards: Encrypted Mastercard, Visa, and Verve payments processed via PCI-DSS Level 1 certified gateways.
  • POS Terminal on Delivery: Our dispatch riders carry verified wireless POS terminals supporting instant card transactions and NIBSS transfer confirmations.
How fast is delivery within Lagos, Abuja, and nationwide across Nigeria?
Next-Day Dispatch Target

Our dispatch speeds are optimized across three regional zones:

  • Lagos Metro Area (Ikeja, Lekki, Victoria Island, Surulere, Yaba): Same-Day Delivery if ordered before 12:00 PM; guaranteed Next-Day delivery for orders placed later.
  • Abuja FCT & Port Harcourt: 1 to 2 Business Days via ExpressAir cargo.
  • Other States Nationwide: 2 to 4 Business Days with GIG Logistics or DHL Express door-to-door tracking.
Are shipments insured against loss or damage during transit?

Every order dispatched from our Ikeja fulfillment center is 100% insured against loss, theft, or physical impact transit damage at zero additional charge to you. Shipments are wrapped in tamper-evident security foil with serial-logged dispatch seals. If any seal appears broken upon arrival, you have the right to reject delivery immediately with zero liability.

What is the return policy if a product has a factory defect?
30-Day Replacement Window

We provide a 30-Day Hassle-Free Replacement & Return Window for any hardware or factory defect. If your device exhibits screen flickering, battery anomalies, or motherboard issues within 30 days of delivery, simply visit our Returns Portal or contact our support desk. We will arrange a free courier pickup or accept drop-offs at our Ikeja verification hub, followed by an instant brand-new unit replacement or full refund within 24 hours of inspection.

Does Nexara provide extended warranty protection beyond manufacturer warranty?

Yes. All laptops, smartphones, and high-end audio hardware include our standard 2-Year Nexara Extended Warranty alongside the standard 1-year Apple/Samsung global manufacturer coverage. Our warranty covers free diagnostic diagnostics, battery health optimization checks, and priority repair servicing at authorized service centers in Lagos and Abuja. Learn more on our Warranty Verification page.

Can I visit your physical verification hub or experience center in Lagos?

Certainly! While we operate primarily as a high-speed digital store, our flagship climate-controlled verification center is located at 14B Admiralty Way, Lekki Phase 1, Lagos (and our technical hub in Ikeja GRA). Customers are welcome to schedule an appointment during business hours (Monday – Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM) to inspect devices, pick up pre-orders, or speak directly with our hardware engineers.

Still Have Questions or Need Custom Sourcing?

Our Nigerian customer success and hardware verification specialists are active 24/7 via WhatsApp, phone, or live chat to answer your inquiries.
