1. Statutory Compliance & Scope

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, processing, storage, and transfer of personal data by Nexara Technologies Nigeria Limited ("Nexara", "We", "Our", or "Us") across our e-commerce web application (`nexaratech.com.ng`), mobile applications, and physical verification centers located in Lagos, Nigeria.

We operate in strict accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), and applicable regulatory directives enforced by the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC). We process all customer data under the principles of lawfulness, fairness, purpose limitation, and absolute transparency.

2. What Personal Data We Actually Collect

To verify order authenticity, execute insured logistics dispatches, and enforce our 2-year hardware warranty, we collect the following specific data categories:

A. Direct Customer Identity & Logistics Data

  • Full Name & Contact Details: First name, last name, email address, and active Nigerian mobile number (required for dispatch rider telematics and OTP delivery confirmation).
  • Physical Destination Coordinates: Street address, landmark, local government area (LGA), and state across Nigeria for order routing (`e.g. Lekki Phase 1, Lagos` or `Wuse 2, Abuja`).
  • Hardware Registration Metadata: When you purchase a device, we associate the specific serial number, IMEI number, and warranty activation timestamp with your customer account profile.

B. Technical Browsing & Device Telemetry

  • Browser & Session Data: IP address, device operating system, screen resolution, and session timestamps to prevent automated bot scraping and credential stuffing attacks.
  • E-Commerce Interaction State: Items placed in your shopping cart, saved wishlists, applied search filters, and product review ratings.

3. Payment Verification & Financial Tokenization

We NEVER collect, store, or transmit your raw 16-digit debit/credit card numbers, expiry dates, or CVV security codes on Nexara servers.

All financial transactions executed through our online checkout are tokenized and processed exclusively by **PCI-DSS Level 1 Certified Payment Gateways** (`Paystack Nigeria` and `Flutterwave`). When you select online card payment or direct bank transfer, your financial details flow directly through 256-bit SSL encrypted tunnels to the central interbank switch (`NIBSS / CBN regulated switches`).

Pay-on-Delivery (PoD) POS Security

When paying via wireless POS terminal upon delivery in Lagos or Abuja, your card PIN is entered directly into our dispatch rider's bank-registered terminal. Nexara does not store terminal transaction logs beyond the automated NIBSS transaction approval receipt code (`RRN`).

4. How We Use Your Personal Data

Your personal information is processed strictly for legitimate operational purposes:

  • Logistics Dispatch & Live Tracking: Transmitting your delivery address and contact number to assigned dispatch couriers (`ExpressLogistics NG`, `GIG Logistics`) to enable real-time telemetry and package handover verification.
  • Warranty Registration & Serial Lookups: Enabling you to check device authenticity and claim 2-year warranty servicing via our IMEI/Serial lookup portal (`/warranty.html`).
  • Fraud Prevention & Anti-Money Laundering (AML): Verifying high-ticket electronics orders (`₦1M+ MacBooks and iPhones`) to prevent identity theft and fraudulent card chargebacks.
  • Customer Support & Dispute Resolution: Reviewing written reviews, order tracking logs, and return requests submitted to our fulfillment team.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing & Zero-Sale Guarantee

Nexara explicitly guarantees that we do NOT sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal data to third-party data brokers, advertising networks, or external marketing agencies.

We share strictly necessary customer data only with trusted infrastructure partners under strict Data Processing Agreements (DPAs):

  • Certified Logistics Providers: (`ExpressLogistics NG`, `GIG Logistics`, `DHL Express`) receive your name, address, and phone number solely to complete final-mile delivery.
  • Licensed Payment Gateways: (`Paystack`, `Flutterwave`) receive transaction reference IDs and order totals to verify bank settlement.
  • Statutory Authorities: We disclose records only when served with a valid court order or formal directive from the Nigeria Police Force (NPF Cybercrime Unit), EFCC, or NDPC investigating stolen electronics or financial crime.

6. Technical Architecture & Data Storage

Our application is fully integrated with a secure Django Rest Framework (DRF) backend and PostgreSQL database. Your shopping cart items, saved wishlists, order history, and product reviews are all securely transmitted to and stored on our remote backend servers in Tier-3 cloud data centers.

We only use your browser's local storage (`localStorage`) to securely maintain your active session token for seamless authentication, and to store non-identifying browsing state such as your recently viewed items. All critical transactional data relies on our encrypted database cluster, ensuring high availability and compliance with strict data protection standards.

7. Your Statutory Rights Under the NDPA 2023

Under Nigerian data protection law, you hold the following non-negotiable rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You may request a complete digital copy of all personal data, order histories, and warranty registration records held under your account profile.
  • Right to Rectification: You may correct inaccurate delivery addresses, phone numbers, or billing profiles directly via your Account settings or support desk.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You may request permanent deletion of your customer profile and personal identifiers from our systems, save for mandatory invoice records required by Nigerian tax and statutory accounting laws (6-year retention period).
  • Right to Restrict Processing & Object: You can opt out of promotional product launch SMS or newsletter bulletins at any time with a single click.
