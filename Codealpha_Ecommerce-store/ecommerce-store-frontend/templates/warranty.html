The 21-Point Verification Standard

Before any smartphone, laptop, or gadget is sealed for dispatch from our Ikeja hub, our hardware engineers complete five core diagnostic stages:

1. Global Serial & IMEI Validation

Direct API check against Apple GSX, Samsung Warranty, and ASUS global registries to confirm clean, non-activated factory status.

2. Battery Cell & Thermal Stress Check

Verification of 100% initial battery capacity with zero charge cycles, alongside full-load thermal dissipation testing.

3. Display & Optical Uniformity

Precision optical scanning for zero dead pixels, TrueTone/OLED color calibration accuracy, and touch digitizer responsiveness.

4. Nigerian Network & Connectivity

Testing across all Nigerian 4G/5G spectrum bands (MTN, Airtel, Globacom) plus Wi-Fi 6E/Bluetooth 5.3 signal strength validation.

5. Port Integrity & Security Sealing

USB-C/Thunderbolt power draw verification followed immediately by application of our tamper-evident holographic serial seal.

Certified Nexara Assurance

Every verified unit receives a digital verification certificate linked permanently to your account profile upon delivery.
