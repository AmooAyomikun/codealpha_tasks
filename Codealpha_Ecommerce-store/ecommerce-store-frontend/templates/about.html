Our Story

Curated for Tech Perfectionists

Nexara was built on a simple premise: Nigeria deserves a premium electronics retailer where authenticity, clean aesthetic, and elite performance come standard.

Engineering Confidence into Every Purchase

In a market cluttered with grey imports, counterfeit accessories, and inflated prices, finding reliable high-end electronics shouldn't be a gamble. We started Nexara in Lagos with direct relationships to global distributors, ensuring that whether you order a flagship smartphone, a professional workstation, or studio-grade audio, what arrives in your hands is 100% genuine and sealed.

Our curation team rigorously screens every device for performance, build precision, and longevity. We strip away the noise and focus on what truly matters: top-tier hardware backed by transparent pricing, lightning-fast delivery across Nigeria, and a two-year warranty you can actually rely on.

Our Mandate

Democratizing Access to Verified Global Hardware.

We built Nexara to solve the single greatest friction point in Nigerian tech retail: trust. By eliminating middleman markup and grey-market supply chains, we ensure that every professional, developer, and creator across Lagos and Abuja receives factory-sealed devices backed by ironclad 2-year warranties and zero-risk Pay on Delivery.
