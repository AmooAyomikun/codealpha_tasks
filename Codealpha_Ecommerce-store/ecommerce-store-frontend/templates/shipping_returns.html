30-Day Hassle-Free Returns & Replacement

We stand firmly behind our 21-point hardware verification. If your product exhibits any factory defect, hardware anomaly, or display malfunction within 30 days of receipt, we provide an expedited replacement process without bureaucratic delays.

1

Log Return Request

Enter your Order ID on our contact desk or WhatsApp support line explaining the observed hardware issue.

2

Free Courier Pickup

Our dispatch courier picks up the boxed item at your address (or drop off at our Ikeja verification hub).

3

Hardware Diagnostics

Our certified engineers run full diagnostics within 24 hours to confirm the factory or component defect.

4

Instant Resolution

A brand-new factory-sealed replacement unit is dispatched immediately or a 100% bank refund is issued.

Important Return Conditions:

  • Items must be returned with their original manufacturer packaging, user manuals, and supplied charging accessories.
  • Accidental physical impact damage, liquid immersion on non-waterproof ports, or unauthorized third-party repairs void the 30-day replacement eligibility.
  • For Pay-on-Delivery orders, customers are encouraged to verify device specifications and physical condition before completing terminal authorization.

Tamper-Evident Packaging & Transit Assurance

Zero Seal-Breach Liability
