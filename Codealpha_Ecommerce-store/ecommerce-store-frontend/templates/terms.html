3. Pay on Delivery (PoD) Legal Definitions & Customer Obligations

To eliminate online shopping anxiety for Nigerian consumers, we offer zero-prepayment **Pay on Delivery (PoD)** across metropolitan zones of Lagos (`Ikeja, Lekki, VI, Surulere, Yaba`) and Abuja FCT. Selecting PoD at checkout creates specific legal rights and binding obligations for both parties:

A. Customer Inspection Rights Prior to Payment

When our dispatch courier arrives at your destination address, **you have the absolute legal right to:**

  • Inspect the outer shipping box and verify that the Nexara holographic security seal is intact and untampered.
  • Examine the outer manufacturer box specification labels (RAM, Storage, Color, Model Number).
  • Verify the printed IMEI or Serial Number on the product box against official manufacturer lookup websites (`checkcoverage.apple.com`) before authorizing card or cash transfer.

Prohibition on Breaking Manufacturer Shrink-Wrap Before Payment

Because opening factory shrink-wrap instantly depreciates high-ticket electronics (`e.g. ₦4M+ MacBooks and iPhones`) from "Brand New Factory Sealed" to "Open Box", **customers are strictly prohibited from cutting open factory shrink-wrap, breaking internal manufacturer security seals, or powering on devices until full payment has been confirmed via POS terminal card swipe, NIBSS bank transfer, or cash**.

B. Unjustified PoD Refusal & Restocking Liability

If a customer breaks a manufacturer's factory shrink-wrap and subsequently refuses to complete payment without demonstrating an objective physical discrepancy or serial number mismatch, the customer shall be legally liable for an **immediate ₦25,000 open-box restocking fee** payable to the dispatch rider. Furthermore, customers who repeatedly log PoD orders and refuse delivery upon arrival (`2 or more unexcused cancellations`) will have their PoD privileges permanently revoked across all Nigerian IP addresses.

C. Dispatch Rider Protocol & Safety

Our dispatch riders are instructed to wait a maximum of **20 minutes** upon arrival at the destination address. If a customer is unreachable via phone/WhatsApp after 20 minutes, the order will be marked "Delivery Failed" and returned to our Ikeja verification hub. Riders are strictly authorized to depart immediately without handover if they encounter verbally abusive environments or unsafe drop-off locations.
